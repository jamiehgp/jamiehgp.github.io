    <h3>About Me</h3>

    <p>

      Jamie Berger is the Senior Innovation Educator at

      <span style="color: black"> The Hidden Genius Project, Detroit </span>. As

      an Ann Arbor native with family roots in Detroit, Jamie has returned to

      Michigan to fulfill her mission of improving the pipeline of young, Black

      people into the field of technology innovation. While completing her

      Masters in Computer Science at Michigan Technological University, Jamie

      felt the direct impact of the lack of diversity. To change that impact,

      Jamie moved to Washington D.C. to teach computer science to high school

      students of color while simultaneously earning her Masters in Education

      from Johns Hopkins University. Throughout her educational process, Jamie

      constructed a passion for creating culturally responsive educational

      content that emphasizes learners' interests to keep them engaged and

      motivated. Jamie hopes to continue inspiring Black youth to have a seat at

      the table in future technology innovations that may impact the globe.

    </p>

    <p>

      In her freetime, Jamie enjoys getting tattoos, watching TV (Naruto,

      reality TV, crime dramas), going to the skatepark, and playing with her

      new baby girl!

    </p>
